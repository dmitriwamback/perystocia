Day of Armistice
Day of Armistice is set in a dystopian future following the Anopol Peace Treaty of 2101, which divided the Federation of Vjsda. The treaty has led to economic recession, geopolitical disputes, and governance issues, sparking revolutions in Mnizh, M'vniki, and Umnsk. Vjsda and Perijstocia were involved in orchestrating conflicts abroad, further destabilizing the region.
Geopolitics plays a role therein. The political rhetoric has shifted from Vjsda to Perijstocia as the sole governing "police" of the region. The inauguration of SWAH'G and SRSQ plagues the region with sophisticated terrorist attacks that threaten global peace.
The government of Perijstocia, under the secretive QDR, uses a range of tactics and schemes to coerce any foreign political ambition that goes against Perijstocia's narrative. The partition of Azortozha's capital Rivynskgorod accorded by the Treaty of Gorovask 1940 caused violent resistance against Perijstocia with the rise of RJOTA.
From February 2nd to March 4th 2103, Perijstocia holds its 2nd anniversary of the signing of the Treaty of Anopol. But this time, regional events - including terrorism - challenge Perijstocia's use of diplomacy, military strategy, foreign affairs and espionage as a weapon of destabilization.
Perijstocia's narrative uses conflicting information to silence its people. It deletes the memories of citizens with implanted cognitive chips that have a view outside of QDR's reach.

Vyteria-Zabčrograd, 1940
Situated on the border between Perystocja and Azortoja, the city state of Vyteria-Zabčrograd has been the center of tensions. The city state was informally integrated into Azortoja, holding a grip on the Navoriya River.
On November 4th 1939, the Convention of Rivynskgorod prohibited the use of military force which threatens the members of CMEA. From February 1940 to December 1940, Perystocja violated this Convention, this led to the creation of the CVKKRRSQZ and KIRTU to counter Perystocja's terrorism in Azortoja.

Day of Armistice: SRSQ
Originating from the Al-Raya al-Khadra group, SRSQ (Sovereign Republic of Snzaq and Qair) wishes to separate and form their own state.
Since the end of the 1980s, the country of Snzaq was governed by the Liga Spravedlivosti Party. In the decades to come, the vast desertic states of east of the country: Al-Mahjar, Nahran, and Qadira all fell into drought. The eastern states became disconnected with the rest of the federation of Snzaq. Piracy and terrorism ensued in the region, without help of ever gaining nation-wide recognition.
Al-Raya al-Khadra, formed in Nahran in 2001, started as a political party who sought diplomatic and economic ties with the majority Liga Spravedlivosti Party.
