Day of Armistice
Day of Armistice is set in a dystopian future following the Anopol Peace Treaty of 2101, which divided the Federation of Vjsda. The treaty has led to economic recession, geopolitical disputes, and governance issues, sparking revolutions in Mnizh, M'vniki, and Umnsk. Vjsda and Perijstocia were involved in orchestrating conflicts abroad, further destabilizing the region.
Geopolitics plays a role therein. The political rhetoric has shifted from Vjsda to Perijstocia as the sole governing "police" of the region. The inauguration of SWAH'G and SRSQ plagues the region with sophositcated terrorist attacks that threaten global peace.
The government of Perijstocia, under the secretive QDR, uses a range of tactics and schemes to coerce any foreign political ambition that goes against Perijstocia's narrative. The partition of Azortozha's capital Rivynskgorod accorded by the Treaty of Gorovask 1940 caused violent resistance against Perijstocia with the rise of RJOTA.
From February 2nd to March 4th 2103, Perijstocia holds its 2nd anniversary of the signing of the Treaty of Anopol. But this time, regional events - including terrorism - challenge Perijstocia's use of diplomacy, military strategy, foreign affairs and espionage as a weapon of destabilization.
Perijstocia's narrative uses conflicting information to silence its people. It deletes the memories of citizens with implanted cognitive chips that have a view outside of QDR's reach.
SRSQ: Sovereign Republic of Snzaq and Qair location: Al Hrqatyr, Snzaq
SWAH'G: Sovereignty of Wkrow-Arijat-Havknat'Gzeva location: Eulkshuizw, Mnizh (?)
RJOTA: Rivynskgorod Junta Organization Towards Accession location: Rivynskgorod, Azortozha
QDR: Queried, Developed, Revolutionized/Regionalized location: Anopol, Perijstocia

Vyteria-Zabčrograd, 1940
Situated on the border between Perystocja and Azortoja, Vyteria-Zabčrograd has been the center of tensions. Perystocja's constant bombardments in Zabčrograd, from February 1940 to December 1940, disturbs regional peace which led to the creation of the CVKKRRSQZ and KIRTU to counter Perystocja's terrorism in Azortoja.
On November 4th 1939, the Convention of Rivynskgorod prohibited the use of military force which threatens the members of CMEA.
CVKKRRSQZ: Coalition of Vidsvonje, Krv'ny, Kvitia, Rsunia, Rusinia, Sbia, Qair, and Zhapatna location: Avakkhanagan, Vidsvonje
KIRTU: Khia International Reform Towards Unification location: Moniya, Rsunia
CMEA: Central Military and Economic Union location: Karakova, Koradin
+Technologies: OpenGL 4.1, MetalKit
Miyuki-Dražen
Following the unification of Fautai and the District of Mikonoma in January of 2000, the city of Kogarashima heavily opposed the unification. The Miyuki-Dražen Accord was signed between the two parties to offer more autonomy to Kogarashima.
By this point, the countries of Krv'ny, Kvitia, Rsunia, Rusinia, and Sbia as well as other city-states agreed to unite into the Yokovnoral Federal Republic of Khia. Behind the scenes, the Fautai government worked in collusion with this newly formed Khia to crack down on Kogarashima. In response, the citizens of Kogarashima held massive riots and orchestrated terrorist attacks against the Fautai government in Mikonotsu.
The Republic of Vidsvonje, Qair and Perystocja intervened to calm the situation, but this further abstracted the Miyuki-Dražen Accord. The divide between the federal government and the rioters grew exponentially, resulting into multiple casualties and the increase of wounded civilians.
As an ambassador to the Pact of Unity, you call upon help to the resolve the Miyuki-Dražen Accord and suppress any new form of resistance.
Yokovnoral: Coalition government between, Krv'ny, Kvitia, Rsunia, Rusinia, and Sbia to unite into Khia location: Moniya, Rsunia
+Technologies: OpenGL 4.1, MetalKit
